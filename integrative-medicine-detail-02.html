吾循－功能醫學

Treatment Overview 療程簡介

明明健檢正常，
卻總覺得身體一直在慢慢耗損。

早上起床還是累、
注意力變差、情緒變鈍，
睡再久都像沒真正休息過。

有時候是腸胃不舒服、反覆發炎、失眠、頭痛，
有時候則是一種說不上來的疲憊感。

你知道自己不太對勁，
卻又很難真正說清楚。

在吾循這個療程，
我們不只看「有沒有生病」，
而是重新檢視睡眠、壓力、腸道、發炎、荷爾蒙與能量代謝之間的失衡。

很多長期的不舒服，
其實不是突然發生的，
而是身體在高壓與失衡下，
慢慢失去了修復能力。

希望帶給妳的，
不是只壓下症狀，
而是陪你重新找回真正穩定的身體狀態。

Functional Wellness
Sometimes, not feeling your best isn't a diagnosis—it's a sign that something is out of balance.
When energy feels depleted, sleep no longer restores, and your body struggles to keep up, it's time to look beyond the symptoms.
By exploring the connections between stress, recovery, metabolism, inflammation, and hormonal health, we help restore balance from within.
Because lasting wellness begins when the body regains its natural ability to heal.

Gut Health
A healthy gut is the foundation of overall well-being.

By supporting digestive balance, nutrient absorption, and the body's natural ecosystem, we help restore harmony from within and support lasting vitality.

Metabolic Balance
Sustainable change begins from within.
